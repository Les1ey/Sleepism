睡眠十诫 The Ten Commandments of Sleep

第一诫：远离酒精 1st: Abstain from Alcohol

完全戒除酒精。如必须饮酒，限制在下午6点前一杯。酒精是最影响睡眠质量的可控因素。

Avoid alcohol completely. If absolutely necessary, limit to one drink before 6 PM. Alcohol is the most significant controllable factor affecting sleep quality.

第二诫：控制进食 2nd: Mind Your Meals

睡前至少三小时不进食。保持轻微饥饿感入睡最佳。

Don't eat within three hours of bedtime. It's best to go to bed slightly hungry.

第三诫：远离电子设备 3rd: Electronic Abstinence

睡前两小时避免使用电子设备。如必须使用，请开启防蓝光模式。

Avoid electronic devices two hours before bed. If necessary, use blue light reduction settings.

第四诫：心灵平静 4th: Mental Peace

睡前一小时避免任何引起焦虑的活动，如查看工作邮件或社交媒体。

Avoid anxiety-inducing activities like checking work emails or social media for at least an hour before bed.

第五诫：温度调节 5th: Temperature Control

睡前泡澡或桑拿，让身体在凉爽的床上自然降温，促进入睡。

Take a hot bath or sauna before bed. The cooling process in a cool bed signals sleep time to your brain.

第六诫：凉爽环境 6th: Cool Environment

保持房间温度在20度左右，使用凉爽的床垫或温控设备。

Keep room temperature in the mid-sixties. Use a cool mattress or bed-cooling device.

第七诫：完全黑暗 7th: Complete Darkness

房间需要完全遮光，最好暗到看不见手掌。必要时使用眼罩。

Make the room completely dark. Use an eye mask if necessary.

第八诫：充足时间 8th: Adequate Time

确保有8-9小时的睡眠机会。给自己充足的睡眠时间。

Give yourself 8-9 hours of sleep opportunity. Budget enough time for adequate sleep.

第九诫：固定作息 9th: Fixed Wake Time

保持固定的起床时间，包括周末。如需灵活，可以调整就寝时间。

Maintain a fixed wake-up time, even on weekends. If needed, vary bedtime instead.

第十诫：放松心态 10th: Don't Obsess

不要过分关注睡眠。避免看闹钟，不要执着于睡眠追踪数据。

Don't obsess over sleep. Avoid clock-watching and don't worry too much about sleep tracking data.
